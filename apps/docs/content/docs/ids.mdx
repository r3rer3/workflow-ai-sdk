---
title: IDs
description: runId, threadId, and resourceId stay explicit because they matter operationally.
---

The library keeps three IDs explicit because they matter operationally:

- `runId` — one workflow execution
- `threadId` — one conversation thread
- `resourceId` — the business entity the conversation belongs to (a user, customer, workspace member, document owner, etc)

Reuse the ids your app already has and which are safe to be shown in the client. Do not invent a new `resourceId` space inside route handlers.
